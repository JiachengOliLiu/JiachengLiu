About Me

Ever since I was doing my undergraduate study in Tongji University, Shanghai, I’ve been passionate about Design, and this passion led me to School of Art and Design, ANU, in which I did my Master of Design programme. My 6 years’ study in both institutions helped me gain rich knowledge of Graphic Design, UIX Design, Photography, Videography and Animation. After I graduated from ANU in 2019, I immediately started my career as a Graphic and Digital Designer at Núcleo Creative & Marketing Agency, in which I obtained a lot of experience doing real projects.
