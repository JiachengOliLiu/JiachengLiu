Brochure Design for Nowra Business Park

Nowra Business Park was a new-built industrial district in Nowra, a town located to the south of Wollongong. I designed an A4-sized brochure for the client to advertise their business venues for rent.

The font sizes, line spacings and paragraph paddings are carefully adjusted.

One important step of brochure design (and any other design for print media) is the exportation of print files. I made sure to flatten all the graphics and texts, setup bleedings and trim lines, and convert all the images to CMYK mode, so that the final printed result looked the same as the design proof that the client had received.
