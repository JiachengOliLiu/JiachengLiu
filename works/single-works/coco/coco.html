Rebranding for COCO Fresh Tea & Juice

This is a rebranding project for COCO Fresh Tea & Juice, a Taiwanese tea company which is famous for its milk tea and juice. Based on its current branding, I redesigned its logo, together with some patterns and illustrations, which were applied in stationeries, menus, posters and packages.
