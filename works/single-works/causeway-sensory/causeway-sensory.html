Website Design for Causeway Sensory

Causeway Sensory is an international company producing multi-sensory products aiming to enhance learning for all age groups. Collaborating with our team at Núcleo, I delivered a refreshing design for their new website.

Before we started the project, our client, Causeway Sensory had got a newly refreshed set of branding design. I reviewed their new brand guidelines carefully and planned the new website to match well with their branding. I firstly hand-drew wireframes to set the website structure, then proceeded onto Figma to create the design mockup. I made sure every section is easy to read for the users and doable in development. I created a 15-page design mockup, including mobile views, and reviewed the developers’ work regularly during the development phase to make sure the site in progress matched my design. At the end we delivered a satisfactory result that is user friendly and visually pleasing.
