Branding Design and UIX Design for Sqimple

Sqimple is an all-in-one enterprise eCommerce platform developed by Núcleo. It features a well-rounded back-end web app which allows online shop owners to manage their orders, inventory, website and marketing strategies. I worked on Sqimple’s branding design as well as the UIX design for the web app.

Branding Design

Sqimple’s target user group is young or middle-aged Australian entrepreneurs and small business owners who are aiming to manage their online orders in a simpler way. Therefore, I created a concise yet vibrant visual identity. Considering our web app was going to have a lot of different sections, I organised a thorough colour palette for convenient usage.

Web App & Mobile App

Sqimple’s online shop management app is where my UIX design skills got to shine. I applied a user-centric approach, trying to find the best solutions that could make shop owners track their orders in the easiest way. I collaborated with our team at Núcleo, and communicated closely with our developers, making sure that my design was doable and user-friendly.

Apart from the web app, I also designed a mobile app for inventory management, as well as Sqimple’s official website.
