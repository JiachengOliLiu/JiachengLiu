The Designer

My long last passion for art and design led me to School of Art and Design, ANU, where I did my Master of Design programme. After I graduated in 2019, I started my career as a Digital Designer at Núcleo Pty Ltd, where I focused mostly on UIX & Web Design. During my work at Núcleo, I obtained a lot of experience working with different clients from various industries.

Identified as a problem solver, I value the importance of logical and critical thinking when I start any new design project. I tend to collect any useful information about my clients and users, and try to learn about their needs and expectations thoroughly. This enables me to digest new knowledge and adapt to different circumstances quickly. Also, by thinking with a clear logic, I could work efficiently in trouble shooting and problem solving.

Furthermore, I’m familiar with the essential design tools as well. I’m a fluent user of Figma and the Adobe Creative Suite, and am especially good at Photoshop, Illustrator, XD and InDesign. Being familiar with these softwares allows me to have good control of my productions, and it makes me work efficiently.

With my 6+ years of working experience, I’m confident that I can use my problem-solving skills to handle all sorts of projects.
